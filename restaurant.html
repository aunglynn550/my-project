About This Project

This is food and restaurant shopping web sit more like a shopping cart with a few more unlikely features.restaurant base functionalities such as adding options for food the customer is adding to his cart. And the customer has to select the various sizes of the item if the item is set to have various sizes by Admin. The customer will be able to play with quantity added to the cart throughout the process before the payment is procceeded. Pay Pal is the only payment this web site offer for customers to complete their orders. The customers can check their orders according to their specifi order details.

Frontend Landing Page For Customers

Home Page includes all the products and their options added by the admininstrator. Customers can view all the products according to the categories. Customers don't need to login before the items are added to the cart.
